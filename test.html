ABOUT THIS SITE

Here to help you in aything related to gaming!

This site was meant to help you gamers on what to do when you're stuck. Whether it's getting stuck on a level, needing some advice on gameplay or to become a pro in gaming, you can count on me to help you anytime! Back when I was young, there were times when I really needed someone to tell me how to get past this boss, adapting different gaming styles, stuff like that. Unfortunately, I couldn't find a single person(not many kids in my neighborhood play games) to help me and the internet back then was just getting started for us gamers. I felt tired, fail after fail, on figthing bosses in Dark Souls(oh boy, it sure is challenging!). Therefore, I set out on a quest to help those who are in the same state that I was before, so that they don't lose interest in the game. Game on, gamers!
